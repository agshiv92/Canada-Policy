SUMMARY
This enactment amends the Department of Public Works and
Government Services Act to permit the Minister, in developing
requirements for public works, to allow the use of wood or any
other thing that achieves environmental benefits.

70-71 ELIZABETH II – 1 CHARLES III
CHAPTER 27
An Act to amend the Department of Public Works and
Government Services Act (use of wood)
[Assented to 26th October, 2023]
His Majesty, by and with the advice and consent of
the Senate and House of Commons of Canada,
enacts as follows:
1996, c. 16
Department of Public Works and
Government Services Act
1 Section 7 of the Department of Public Works
and Government Services Act is amended by
adding the following after subsection (1):
Use of wood
(1.1) In developing requirements with respect to the
construction, maintenance and repair of public works,
federal real property and federal immovables, the Minis-
ter shall consider any potential reduction in greenhouse
gas emissions and any other environmental benefits and
may allow the use of wood or any other thing — including
a material, product or sustainable resource — that
achieves such benefits.
